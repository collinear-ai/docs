## 1. Choose and View a Dataset

Select the dataset you’d like to export by clicking directly on the **dataset card**.

<img
  src="/images/selectadataset.png"
  alt="Select a dataset screen"
  style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem",marginTop:"1rem",marginBottom:"1rem" }}
/>

---

## 2. Export the Dataset

Once you're viewing your dataset, click the **Export** button. This will download your data as a `.json` file.

<img
  src="/images/exportadataset.png"
  alt="Click export button"
  style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem",marginTop:"1rem",marginBottom:"1rem" }}
/>

---

That’s it\! You’ve successfully exported your dataset from the Collinear AI Platform.
