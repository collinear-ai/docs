# Steps to Export Dataset

## Access the Dataset Homepage
Navigate to your desired space and select the dataset option from the sidebar.
<img
    style={{borderRadius: '0.5rem'}}
    src="/images/export_dataset/click_on_dataset.png"
/>

## Choose and View a Dataset
Pick the dataset you want to export and click the "View" button.
<img
    style={{borderRadius: '0.5rem'}}
    src="/images/export_dataset/select_a_dataset.png"
/>

## Export the Dataset
Click on the "Export" button to download your dataset as a JSON file.
<img
    style={{borderRadius: '0.5rem'}}
    src="/images/export_dataset/click_export.png"
/>
