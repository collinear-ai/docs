---
title: What is Collinear AI
description: "Collinear AI offers a one-stop platform for post-training, enabling enterprises to customize Large Language Models (LLMs) to their bespoke criteria.

The API provides access to Collinear's proprietary post-training technologies:

1. AI Judges for evaluating LLMs across three key dimensions:
   - Safety
   - Reliability
   - Bespoke Quality criteria

2. Synthetic data generation and curation capabilities

3. Auto-Align technology for fine-tuning models

Developers can leverage these tools to create custom AI models that:
- Comply with application-specific safety requirements
- Ensure factual accuracy
- Maintain brand alignment

The API supports various integration options and includes comprehensive documentation to facilitate seamless implementation. With Collinear's API, businesses can unlock the full potential of AI technologies while addressing the current limitations of off-the-shelf models."
